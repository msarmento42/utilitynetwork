# Top 5 AI Tools for Creators

Creating content is harder than ever with the demand for volume. Here are the tools we use daily.

1. **Midjourney**: For thumbnails and asset generation.
2. **RunwayML**: For video editing and background removal.
3. **ElevenLabs**: For voiceovers that sound human.
4. **Descript**: For editing video by editing text.
5. **ChatGPT**: For scripting and ideation.

## Why Use Them?

> "It's not about replacing creativity, it's about removing the friction between idea and execution."

Start with one, master it, then add another.
