## What you'll accomplish

In this workflow, you'll use AI to generate ideas and organize them into a social media posting schedule. By following these steps, you will have a weekly or monthly social media calendar filled with post topics, captions, and suggested hashtags. This ensures a steady flow of content on your social platforms and keeps your branding consistent, without having to brainstorm each day.

## Who this is for

This guide is for social media managers, marketing teams, solopreneurs, or small business owners handling their own social channels. If you manage social platforms and need help coming up with regular post content (for example, planning posts on Instagram, Twitter, or LinkedIn), this workflow will save you time. You should have a basic idea of your brand and target audience. The AI will generate post ideas, but you'll review and customize them. No special design skills are needed, just creativity and a clear sense of your brand voice.

## Inputs

Get these ready:
- **Brand and audience:** Describe your brand voice and the audience or platform (e.g., "Casual tech brand, targeting young developers on Twitter").
- **Content themes or topics:** List themes to cover (product updates, industry tips, behind-the-scenes, user stories, etc.).
- **Posting frequency:** How often you post (daily, 3 times a week, etc.) and the time frame for the calendar (one week, one month).
- **Key messages or calls-to-action:** Any specific messages, promotions, or hashtags to include.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. **Define content pillars.** Identify 3-5 core themes (pillars) for your content (e.g. "Education," "Inspiration," "Promotion").
2. **Generate post ideas.** Ask the AI for ideas under each pillar.
   ```
   Generate 5 social media post ideas for a [Brand] about [Theme]. Include caption hooks and visual ideas.
   ```
3. **Draft captions.** Ask the AI to write the actual post text.
   ```
   Write an Instagram caption for a post about [Topic]. Use a [Tone] voice and include relevant hashtags.
   ```
4. **Schedule into a calendar.** Assign each post to a date and time based on your frequency. You can do this manually or with AIâ€™s help:
   ```
   Create a weekly posting schedule with these ideas, alternating between themes.
   ```
5. **Review and refine.** Check that the mix of content is balanced and the tone is consistent. Add images or videos to match the captions.

## Prompt templates

- **Brainstorming:**
  ```
  "Give me 10 creative social media post ideas for [Industry/Brand] related to [Holiday/Event]."
  ```
- **Caption writing:**
  ```
  "Write a compelling LinkedIn post about [Topic]. Keep it professional yet conversational. End with a question to drive engagement."
  ```
- **Hashtag suggestions:**
  ```
  "Suggest 10 relevant hashtags for a post about [Topic] on [Platform]."
  ```
- **Calendar structure:**
  ```
  "Organize these 10 post ideas into a 2-week content calendar, posting every weekday."
  ```

## Common mistakes

- **Robotic tone:** AI can sound stiff. Always add your personal flair or emojis to make it sound human.
- **Ignoring platform norms:** Don't use hashtags on Facebook the same way you do on Instagram. Specify the platform in your prompt.
- **Repetitive content:** Ensure you vary the types of posts (video, image, text) so your feed doesn't look monotonous.

## Quality checklist

- The calendar has a good mix of content types (educational, promotional, fun).
- Captions are engaging and fit the platform's character limit.
- Hashtags are relevant and not spammy.
- The schedule is realistic for your team to maintain.
